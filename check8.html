Service Overview

Why Orthodontic Treatment Matters

Crooked, crowded, protruding, or unevenly spaced teeth can affect more than appearance. They may also make cleaning difficult, increase wear on teeth, and contribute to bite problems or jaw discomfort.

Orthodontic treatment gradually moves teeth into better alignment. At our dental clinic in Goregaon West, we help patients choose between braces and aligners based on case complexity, visibility preferences, and daily routine.

Alignment That Looks Good & Functions Well

The goal is not just straighter teeth, but a healthier bite, easier cleaning, and long-term smile stability.

Common Concerns

Signs You May Need Braces or Aligners

↔️

Crowded Teeth

Teeth overlapping or turning out of place can affect both aesthetics and oral hygiene.

📏

Gaps Between Teeth

Spaces may affect smile harmony and sometimes indicate bite or tooth size concerns.

😬

Protruding Teeth

Forward-positioned teeth may affect appearance, lip closure, and trauma risk.

⚖️

Bite Problems

Overbite, underbite, crossbite, or open bite may affect chewing and tooth wear.

🪥

Hard to Clean Areas

Misaligned teeth can create spots that are difficult to brush or floss properly.

Treatment Process

How Orthodontic Planning Works

1

Consultation & Examination

Your alignment, bite, jaw relation, and smile goals are assessed in detail.

2

Records & Planning

Photos, scans, X-rays, or other records may be used to understand your orthodontic needs properly.

3

Choose the Right System

Based on your case and preferences, braces or aligners are recommended.

4

Active Tooth Movement

Teeth are gradually guided into better positions through regular monitoring and adjustments.

5

Retention

After treatment, retainers help maintain the new alignment and protect the final result.
