Invisalign and traditional braces both straighten teeth but differ significantly. Traditional metal braces use brackets bonded to teeth connected by wires, are visible, fixed, and typically cost ₹45,000-65,000. They work for all orthodontic cases including severe misalignment. Invisalign in Goregaon Mumbai uses clear, removable plastic aligners that are virtually invisible, more comfortable, and allow normal eating and brushing. Invisalign costs ₹1,80,000-2,50,000 and suits mild to moderate cases. Treatment times are similar (12-18 months). Invisalign offers aesthetic advantage, comfort, and convenience but requires patient discipline to wear 22 hours daily. Our certified Invisalign provider helps you choose the best option based on your case complexity, budget, and lifestyle preferences.

Maintaining excellent oral hygiene at home involves consistent daily practices recommended by dentists in Goregaon West. Brush twice daily for 2 minutes using fluoride toothpaste and soft-bristled brush at 45-degree angle. Floss daily to remove plaque between teeth where brushes can't reach. Use antimicrobial mouthwash to reduce bacteria. Replace toothbrush every 3 months. Limit sugary and acidic foods that cause cavities. Drink plenty of water throughout the day. Avoid tobacco products which cause gum disease and oral cancer. Chew sugar-free gum after meals when brushing isn't possible. These habits, combined with regular professional cleanings every 6 months at Dental Studio 32, ensure optimal oral health, prevent dental problems, and maintain a bright, healthy smile.
