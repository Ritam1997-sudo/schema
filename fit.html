Service Overview

What Are Dental Implants?

A dental implant is a titanium post placed into the jawbone to act like the root of a missing tooth. Once healed, it supports a crown, bridge, or denture depending on how many teeth need replacement.

Unlike removable options, implants are fixed, stable, and designed to integrate with the bone. At our dental clinic in Goregaon West, implant treatment is planned with precision so your new tooth feels comfortable and looks natural in your smile.

Replace Missing Teeth with Confidence

Treatment Process

How Implant Treatment Is Planned

1

Consultation & Evaluation

Your oral condition, missing tooth area, and bone support are assessed clinically and radiographically.

2

Treatment Planning

We decide the implant position, number of implants needed, and the most suitable final restoration.

3

Implant Placement

The titanium implant is placed carefully into the bone under controlled surgical conditions.

4

Healing Phase

The implant integrates with the bone over time to create a stable foundation.

5

Final Crown or Prosthesis

A custom-made crown, bridge, or denture is attached to complete the restoration.
