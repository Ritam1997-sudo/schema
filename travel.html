What is Complete Travel & Tourism Schema Markup?

This all-in-one travel and tourism schema generator creates a comprehensive structured data package that covers every aspect of your travel business. Instead of implementing separate schemas for organization, services, packages, and reviews, you get one unified schema that search engines can fully understand.

Our complete schema combines TravelAgency, Service, TouristTrip, AggregateRating, and ContactPoint schemas into a single, interconnected structured data object. This approach provides maximum SEO value by giving search engines a complete picture of your business - from basic contact information to detailed tour packages, pricing, customer reviews, and service areas.

Travel businesses need more than basic organization markup. You need to showcase your tour packages, services, destinations, pricing, customer ratings, payment methods, and unique features. This comprehensive tool generates all of that in one professional, valid JSON-LD schema that's ready to implement on your website.

Fill Out Business Basics

Start with your essential business information - name, type, website URL, description, and contact details. These required fields form the foundation of your schema. Be accurate and match the information visible on your website exactly.

Add Tour Packages & Services

Use the "Add Tour Package" and "Add Service" buttons to include your offerings. Add as many packages and services as you want - each one makes your business more discoverable in search. Include names, descriptions, prices, and durations for best results.

3

Complete All Optional Sections

Fill in operating hours, social media links, ratings, service areas, payment methods, and amenities. While technically optional, these sections dramatically improve your schema's effectiveness. The more complete your schema, the better your search visibility.

4

Generate and Review Schema

Click "Generate Complete Schema" to create your comprehensive JSON-LD markup. Review the output carefully to ensure all information is correct. The preview shows exactly what search engines will see, so verify accuracy before implementation.

5

Implement on Your Website

Copy the generated schema and add it to your website's homepage in the <head> section within <script type="application/ld+json"> tags. Test your implementation using Google's Rich Results Test, then monitor performance in Google Search Console.
