Frequently Asked Questions About Dental Care

Everything you need to know before your first visit to Dental Studio 32, the best dental clinic in Goregaon West Mumbai.

Dental Studio 32 is widely regarded as the best dental clinic in Goregaon West, Mumbai, with a 4.9 Google rating from 500+ reviews. Located near Shastri Nagar and Link Road, we offer the full range of dental treatments — from teeth cleaning and root canal to implants and smile makeovers — all by MDS-qualified specialists in a modern, hygienic facility.

At Dental Studio 32, root canal treatment (RCT) in Goregaon West typically costs between ₹3,500 and ₹8,000 per tooth, depending on the number of canals and the tooth's location. Front teeth are generally less complex than back molars. We use rotary endodontics for faster, pain-free single-sitting RCT wherever possible. A personalised quote is given after your clinical examination.

Dental implant costs in Mumbai at Dental Studio 32 range from ₹25,000 to ₹60,000 for a single-tooth implant (including the implant fixture, abutment, and crown). The variation depends on the implant brand, bone condition, and whether a bone graft is required. We offer 0% EMI options to make implants affordable for everyone in Goregaon West and across Mumbai.

Yes, professional teeth whitening at a qualified dental clinic is completely safe. At Dental Studio 32, we use clinically approved, ADA-compliant whitening agents administered by trained professionals. Compared to over-the-counter products, our in-office whitening is more effective (up to 8 shades brighter) and far safer because the gums are protected with a barrier gel throughout the procedure. Some patients experience mild sensitivity for 24–48 hours, which resolves on its own.

Most dental professionals, including our team at Dental Studio 32, recommend a dental check-up and professional cleaning every 6 months. However, if you have a history of gum disease, dental decay, or wear braces or dentures, more frequent visits (every 3–4 months) may be advised. Regular visits help catch problems early, before they become costly or painful to treat.

Both braces and Invisalign can correct crooked teeth, gaps, and bite issues effectively. Traditional braces are often more suitable for complex cases and are generally more affordable (₹18,000–₹35,000). Invisalign is nearly invisible, removable, and more comfortable — making it popular with working professionals and teens — but is typically more expensive (₹80,000–₹1,50,000). At Dental Studio 32, our orthodontist will assess your teeth and recommend the best option for your specific case during a free consultation.

Dental implants are titanium screws that are surgically placed into the jawbone to replace missing tooth roots. A crown is then attached on top, looking and functioning exactly like a natural tooth. With proper oral hygiene and regular dental visits, implants can last a lifetime — making them the gold standard for tooth replacement. At Dental Studio 32 in Goregaon West, we use internationally certified implant systems with a proven long-term success rate above 97%.

We use topical anaesthetic gels before any injection, ultra-thin needles, slow-delivery anaesthesia techniques, and advanced rotary instruments that significantly reduce procedural discomfort. The vast majority of our patients — including many who previously had dental phobia — report being completely comfortable throughout their treatment.
