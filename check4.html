Service Overview

Which Option Is Right for You?

Crowns are used to cover and protect weak or heavily restored teeth. Bridges replace one or more missing teeth by joining artificial teeth to supporting natural teeth or implants. Veneers are thin cosmetic coverings placed on the front surface of teeth to improve shape, colour, and symmetry.

At our dental clinic in Goregaon West, we recommend the right option based on tooth condition, smile goals, and long-term durability. If you want stronger teeth or a more polished smile from an experienced dentist in Goregaon West, this treatment category offers highly versatile solutions.

Treatment Options

Understanding Crowns, Bridges & Veneers

👑

Dental Crowns

Crowns cover damaged or root canal treated teeth to restore strength, shape, and function while protecting the remaining tooth.

🌉

Dental Bridges

Bridges replace missing teeth by filling the gap and helping restore proper chewing, appearance, and bite balance.

✨

Porcelain Veneers

Veneers improve the front appearance of teeth affected by stains, minor gaps, uneven edges, or shape irregularities.

💎

Zirconia & All-Ceramic Options

These materials offer strength, aesthetics, and a more natural tooth-like appearance in visible smile areas.

🧩

Custom Shade Matching

Restorations are selected to blend naturally with your adjacent teeth for a seamless look.

😁

Smile Enhancement

These treatments improve both dental function and smile confidence in a highly predictable way.

Who Needs This Treatment

Common Reasons Patients Choose These Restorations

🦷

Broken or Cracked Tooth

A crown may protect the tooth and restore function if significant structure has been lost.

🛠️

After Root Canal Treatment

Teeth treated with RCT often need a crown for better long-term support and fracture resistance.

❌

Missing Tooth

A bridge can fill the gap and improve chewing, smile balance, and neighbouring tooth support.

🎨

Stained or Discoloured Teeth

Veneers may be recommended when whitening alone cannot deliver the desired result.

📏

Uneven Shape or Minor Gaps

Veneers help correct cosmetic imperfections and create a more symmetrical smile line.

😬

Worn Teeth

Teeth affected by grinding or wear may require restorative coverage to regain function and appearance.

Treatment Process

How the Treatment Is Planned

1

Consultation & Smile Assessment

We examine the tooth or teeth involved and understand whether the goal is functional repair, replacement, cosmetic improvement, or both.

2

Preparation

The tooth is prepared based on the type of restoration needed while preserving healthy structure as much as possible.

3

Impression / Measurement

Accurate records are taken so the restoration fits comfortably and blends well with the natural bite.

4

Custom Fabrication

Your crown, bridge, or veneer is fabricated according to the selected material, size, and colour match.

5

Final Placement

Once ready, the restoration is checked, adjusted if needed, and placed for long-term use and comfort.
