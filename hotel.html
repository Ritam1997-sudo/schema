Our Story

Cooked the way home does it

Kurry Patta started as a single kitchen with one promise: every dish leaves tasting like someone's grandmother made it. That promise hasn't changed, even as the menu grew to span four cuisines.

We grind our own spice blends every morning, source vegetables and herbs daily, and let our chefs cook the way they learned at home — slow curries, hand-rolled dosa batter, charcoal tandoors, and a wok that never stops moving.

  • Fresh ingredients daily
  • Traditional family recipes
  • Passionate, trained chefs
  • Warm, family hospitality
