Our Story

Charted, not fused.

For centuries, ships carried cardamom out of Kerala, cloves out of Zanzibar, sumac from the Levant and saffron from Persia along the same monsoon winds. Spice Route keeps that itinerary alive on one menu — not blended into something new, but plated the way each port has always served it.

Every dish here traces back to a place, a family recipe, and a spice trader who once made the crossing. Pull up a chair. You're joining us mid-voyage.
