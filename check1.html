Service Overview

Why a Dental Consultation Matters

Many dental problems begin quietly. A small cavity, gum inflammation, bite issue, or hidden infection may not always cause pain in the beginning. A professional consultation helps detect these issues early and prevents bigger treatments later.

At our dental clinic in Goregaon West, every consultation is focused on honest diagnosis and practical guidance. If you are looking for a trusted dentist in Goregaon West, this visit gives you complete clarity on what is needed, what can wait, and what treatment options are best for you.

Diagnosis Before Treatment

We believe the best dentistry starts with careful evaluation, clear explanation, and zero guesswork.

What’s Included

Your Consultation May Include

🔍

Detailed Oral Examination

We assess your teeth, gums, bite, existing dental work, and any signs of infection, decay, wear, mobility, or damage.

🩻

Digital RVG X-Ray

When required, digital RVG imaging helps us examine roots, bone levels, deep decay, hidden infection, and impacted teeth more accurately.

📷

Intraoral Camera Analysis

See what we see. Intraoral images make it easier to understand the problem and discuss treatment options clearly.

📝

Treatment Planning

You receive a personalised treatment plan based on your needs, timeline, oral condition, and budget.

⚖️

Second Opinions

If you’ve been advised major treatment elsewhere, we can help you understand whether it is necessary and what alternatives exist.

💬

Clear Guidance

We explain the issue in simple language so you can make an informed decision without confusion or pressure.

Who Should Book This

When You Should Schedule a Consultation

😖

Tooth Pain

If you have pain while chewing, sensitivity, or spontaneous discomfort, a proper diagnosis helps locate the cause early.

🩸

Bleeding Gums

Bleeding, swelling, or bad breath may indicate gum disease and should be evaluated before it worsens.

🦷

Broken or Decayed Tooth

Chips, cavities, dark spots, and worn teeth need assessment before they turn into deeper problems.

😬

Bite or Alignment Issues

If your teeth feel crowded, uneven, or difficult to clean, we can guide you toward the right corrective treatment.

📄

Second Opinion

If another clinic recommended treatment and you want clarity, this visit gives you a calm, professional review.

✅

Routine Check-Up

Even without symptoms, regular dental examinations help detect silent problems and protect your long-term oral health.

Our Process

What Happens During Your Visit

1

Understand Your Concern

We begin by listening to your symptoms, previous dental history, and what you want to improve.

2

Clinical Examination

Your teeth, gums, bite, and surrounding oral structures are checked carefully.

3

Digital Imaging if Needed

RVG X-rays and intraoral images help us assess deeper structures and confirm the diagnosis.

4

Diagnosis & Explanation

We explain what the issue is, what caused it, and whether it needs immediate treatment.

5

Personalised Treatment Plan

You receive a clear recommendation with timelines, treatment options, and estimated costs.
